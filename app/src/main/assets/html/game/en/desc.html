Stock

Life - increases the number of attempts for continuation of a game.
the Chest with bombs - resupplies bombs on 8 units.
Fuel - increases a fuel reserve on 250 units.
Time - increases time stock on 500 units.
the Prize - increases experience on 1000 units.

Environment

Emptiness - free space. Gives the chance to beings to move.
Earth - the filled space. Only of can move in it.
the Stone - a game element. With his help it is possible to kill beings. Pushing, he can be moved in the horizontal direction. It is subject to gravitation and falling, can lead to death.
the Bomb - a game element. It is also possible to push her and it is subject to gravitation too. But in difference from a stone, at collision - blows up. Also some beings, for example at contact with her perish. The bomb is capable to start chain reaction in case in an epitsetra there are explosive objects, or other bombs. This ability needs to be used for faster cleaning of the planet.
Concrete - an element of soil which can't be destroyed. Doesn't give in to destruction, even at impact of close explosion. Stones and bombs soskalzivat from him.
the Brick - a soil element, Is impassable, but from explosion collapses.
Explosion - epicenter of explosion which forms around itself(himself) an explosive cloud and provokes, at an opportunity, chain reaction.
