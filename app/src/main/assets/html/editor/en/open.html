Loading of the planet

All planets available to loading are presented in the list form sorted by a position of planets in system.

In each element of the list are shown:

miniature preview of the planet

name

date of creation

and also two arrows up/down. Shooters allow a raid to change a sequence of planets in system.

In a window of loading it is possible to choose the preset systems of planets. In this case, at the choice of system, planets from it will be shown.

The system in which there is a planet edited at present is by default chosen.

At clique on the planet interesting you, it will be loaded and activated for editing.
